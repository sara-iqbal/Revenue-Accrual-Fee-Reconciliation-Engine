Sheet 1 — Revenue Summary

Annual P&L per fund including gross accrual, fee waivers, net revenue, operating costs, profit, and margin. Primary deliverable for quarterly external reporting disclosures.

Sheet 2 — Monthly Accruals

1,080 rows of monthly accrual data per fund per share class. Includes average AUM, month-end AUM, gross accrual, fee waiver, net revenue, blended fee rate, and trading days.

Sheet 3 — Reconciliation

Full reconciliation output comparing BLK-calculated accruals against external administrator statements, with variance amounts, variance percentages, break flags, and resolution status.

Sheet 4 — Breaks & Exceptions

Filtered view of all breaks above the 0.01% threshold, sorted by absolute variance. Includes root cause classification, resolution status, and escalation flag for audit review.

Sheet 5 — Journal Entries

Complete journal entry ledger with JE ID, period, debit and credit accounts, amounts, and descriptions. Supports statutory audit by providing traceable backups and schedules.

Sheet 6 — P&L Variance

Annual P&L variance analysis comparing BLK totals against administrator totals by fund. Reports total variance in dollars and basis points for management sign-off.
